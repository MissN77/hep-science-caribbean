About the Author

Brenda Hayles is an award-winning science educator and former Vice Principal with over 25 years of teaching and leadership experience.

She is the author and illustrator of the HEP Science primary curriculum, now used in a network of more than 150 schools across England.

Brenda designs and leads national CPD programmes and serves as a district network lead, working with teachers and subject leaders to strengthen curriculum, teaching practice, and leadership.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this digital only?

Yes. Everything is delivered digitally as PowerPoint slides and PDF booklets. You print the student booklets in-house as many times as you need. No shipping costs, no waiting for deliveries, no per-pupil charges.

Do we need specialist science teachers?

No. HEP Science is designed so that any classroom teacher can deliver it confidently. The lesson slides guide the teaching, the booklets structure the learning, and the CPD materials build subject knowledge over time.

Does it align with the Jamaica National Standards Curriculum?

Yes. We have mapped all 36 units against the NSC. You can download the free alignment guide to see the mapping for every grade level.

Is training included?

Yes. Every whole-school licence includes an implementation session with Brenda, plus CPD materials your science lead can use with staff throughout the year.

Can we try before we buy?

Absolutely. Email Brenda to request a free sample unit. You will receive a complete set of lesson slides, a student booklet, and the accompanying vocabulary and assessment materials for one unit.
